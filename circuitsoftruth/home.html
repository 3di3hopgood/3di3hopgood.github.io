CIRCUITS OF TRUTH.

WELCOME TO CIRCUITS OF TRUTH. THIS PROJECT ATTEMPTS TO REVERSE ENGINEER THE MECHANISMS OF TRUTH PRODUCTION BY LOOKING AT THE BROADER DIGITAL INFRASTRUCTURES THEY COMPRISE. IT REFRAMES BLUE TICKS AS AN INFORMATION PROCESSING DEVICE WHOSE FUNCTION IS TO ENCODE AND DECODE SIGNALS. IT FOCUSES ON THE USER INTERACTIONS THAT POWER THIS CIRCUIT, REVEALING HOW ENGAGEMENT RATES DETERMINE WHO OR WHAT IS CONSIDERED 'TRUE'.
